Rui Pan is a first-year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science department at Princeton University, advised by Prof. Ravi Netravali. He does research at the intersection of networked systems and machine learning. Prior to joining Princeton, he had also interned at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, where he worked with Prof. Yiting Xia on networked systems for machine learning. He obtained his B.S. from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 2022, working with Prof. Shivaram Venkataraman on cluster resource management for deep learning workloads.
